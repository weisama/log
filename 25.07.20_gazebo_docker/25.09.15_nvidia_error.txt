docker容器启动工程报错：
Error response from daemon: Cannot restart container px4: could not select device driver "" with capabilities: [[gpu]]
nvidia-container-cli: initialization error: nvml error: driver not loaded

lsmod | grep nvidia → 空
nvidia-smi → 报错 driver not loaded
lspci | grep nvidia → 硬件 RTX 3060 存在
结论：驱动没加载


mokutil --sb-state
如果输出 SecureBoot enabled
说明Secure Boot 阻止内核模块加载

解决方法
重启→F5进入BIOS→高级设置→Securit→Secure Boot→ 关掉。
